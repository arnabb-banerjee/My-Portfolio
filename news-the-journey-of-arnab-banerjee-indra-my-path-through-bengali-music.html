The Journey of Arnab Banerjee (Indra)

My Path Through Bengali Music

📍 Kolkata, India — July 5, 2025

Growing up surrounded by the melodies of Bengali music and the timeless influence of Indian classical training, I found my path not through sudden discovery, but through steady devotion to voice and emotion. My name is Arnab Banerjee, but I perform under the stage name Indra — and music is more than a profession, it’s a lifelong journey.

🎤 My Beginnings

My musical roots are grounded in Hindustani classical training, pursued for over five years. The discipline of ragas gave me strength and flexibility to express emotions beyond words. Unlike many, I did not begin with viral fame or TV shows — but with practice, patience, intimate performances, and countless nights of riyaaz.

🎶 Playback & Film Contributions

My voice entered Bengali cinema through playback singing. I’ve contributed vocals to films such as:

  • Pora Baanshi
  • Swapna Puraner Deshe
  • Ami Rabindranath 2
  • Panta Bhaater Biriyani

🎧 Voice, Vision & Vibe

My sound blends classical emotion with modern sensibility. Influenced by old Bengali melodies, Rabindra Sangeet, and Indian pop/Sufi, I don’t rush music — I feel every note, whether in the studio, on stage, or during midnight practice.

📈 What’s Next?

I continue to train daily and create songs with the goal of leaving behind music that outlives me. To my supporters — thank you. To new listeners — welcome. This is just the beginning.
