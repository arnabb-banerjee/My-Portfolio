Recognition & Achievements of Arnab Banerjee – Bengali Playback Singer & Indie Artist

Top 5 Emerging Bengali Vocalists

Kolkata FM – 2023

In 2023, Arnab Banerjee, also known as Indra, was honored as one of the top 5 emerging Bengali playback singers by Kolkata FM 93.5. His soulful performance of “Ei Shohor” ran for three consecutive weeks, earning high audience engagement.

Guest Performer on Doordarshan Bangla

National Cultural TV Appearance

Arnab Banerjee was featured on Doordarshan Bangla’s cultural program “Tarana.” He showcased original Bengali compositions and a tribute to Rabindranath Tagore, broadcast nationally to millions of viewers.

Featured Artist – Spotify Rising India

Independent Music Achievement

His single “Ei Shohor” was included in Spotify’s Rising India Playlist, crossing over 120,000 streams in just 3 weeks. This recognition placed Arnab among India’s most promising Bengali indie artists.

Playback Acknowledgement – Pora Baanshi

Film Industry Recognition (2021)

In the Bengali film “Pora Baanshi”, Arnab Banerjee’s playback singing was highly praised by critics and featured prominently on JioSaavn and Apple Music.

Indie Artist Spotlight – Amazon Music India

Independent Artist Recognition

In 2023, Amazon Music India featured Arnab Banerjee as part of its “Indie Voices of the East” campaign. His work was highlighted on playlists and the Amazon Music homepage.
