🎤 Arnab Banerjee (Stage Name: Indra)

Playback Singer · Independent Artist · Composer

Artist Biography

Arnab Banerjee, popularly known as Indra, is a Kolkata-based Bengali playback singer, independent artist, and music composer. With over five years of professional training in Indian classical music, he has developed a soulful voice that blends traditional depth with modern cinematic emotion. His versatility allows him to deliver both romantic ballads and fusion-inspired melodies that connect deeply with audiences.

🎬 Contributions to Cinema

  • 🎶 Aasham Rabindranath 2 (2024) – Background Score & Playback Vocals
  • 🎶 Panta Bhander Special (2025) – Upcoming Release, Track: “O Priyo Priyo”
  • 🎶 Stopod Banshi (Expected Sept 2025) – Featured Song: “Amar Bhalobasha Kothay” (Title Track)
  • 🎶 A Dream Fulfilled (2024) – Independent project blending emotional storytelling with soulful music

🎶 Musical Portfolio

Arnab has recorded over 45 playback and independent songs, many of which are admired for their emotional depth and lyrical storytelling. Some fan-favorite tracks include:

  • 🎵 “Prem O Priyo Andhakare”
  • 🎵 “Preme Pore Abaar Preme”
  • 🎵 “Mon Porey Tomar Kotha”
  • 🎵 “Phultuli Gaye Phool Hasiye”
  • 🎵 “Tumi Je Amar Akash”

🎼 Musical Style & Inspirations

Inspired by legends like Mohammed Rafi, Kishore Kumar, and Arijit Singh, Arnab’s music bridges classical roots with modern cinematic soundscapes. His singing is marked by heartfelt emotion and soul-stirring melodies.
