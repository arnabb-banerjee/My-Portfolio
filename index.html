Arnab Banerjee, widely known by his artistic name Indra, is a passionate Indian playback singer, independent music composer, and rising voice in the Bengali music industry. His sound is defined by soulful expression, nuanced classical training, and a bold willingness to experiment with genres. With over 45 officially released tracks on platforms such as Spotify, Amazon Music, Apple Music, JioSaavn, and YouTube, Arnab has carved out a unique space that bridges heritage and innovation.

Born and raised in Kolkata, Arnab's musical journey began early under the tutelage of renowned gurus in Hindustani classical music. His dedication to voice culture and raga-based training laid a solid foundation that reflects in every performance. However, Arnab has never limited himself to traditionalism. He ventured into playback singing for Bengali feature films like Pora Baanshi, Ami Rabindranath 2, and Panta Bhaater Biriyani, delivering melodies that stay with listeners long after the credits roll.

As an independent artist, Arnab is celebrated for his expressive vocal style that resonates with both mainstream and indie audiences. His voice carries a rare emotive depth — blending the sensitivity of ghazals, the structure of classical music, and the vibrancy of modern pop-folk fusion. From soulful ballads to energetic contemporary tracks, his singing adds authenticity and emotional weight to each composition. Songs like Ei Shohor and Swapna Puraner Deshe showcase his vocal range and musical adaptability, and have been featured across major streaming platforms including Spotify and JioSaavn.

Arnab’s music journey is steadily evolving, powered by his own commitment to growth and genuine love for music. While he has not yet performed on national television or been officially ranked by radio broadcasters, his tracks have organically reached thousands of listeners across digital platforms. With over 45 original releases available globally, he continues to build a loyal audience drawn to the authenticity of his voice and lyrical content. Arnab's artistic focus is clear: to create emotionally resonant music that blends heritage, heart, and innovation.

Beyond his musical achievements, Arnab is known for his humility, poetic writing, and deep respect for Bengali language and literature. Whether performing live or producing in his home studio, every project is driven by passion. With a growing fanbase across India and the Bengali diaspora, Arnab Banerjee stands at the forefront of a new generation of Bengali musicians — proud of their roots and fearless in exploring the future.

Recognition

    Arnab Banerjee (Indra) was selected as one of the top 5 emerging Bengali playback singers by Kolkata FM 93.5. His soulful rendition of "Ei Shohor" was broadcast for three consecutive weeks and received exceptional audience feedback. This honor recognized his contribution to modern Bengali music and his unique classical-influenced vocal tone.

The performance was nationally televised and applauded for its emotional depth and live vocal delivery.
  • Featured Artist – Spotify Rising India Playlist
    Arnab Banerjee's independent single “Ei Shohor” was selected by Spotify for the “Rising India” playlist. As a modern Bengali playback singer blending classical training with digital soundscapes, Indra reached over 120,000 streams in just 3 weeks. This exposure placed him among India's most promising regional artists on the platform.

    In the 2021 Bengali film *Pora Baanshi*, Arnab Banerjee’s playback track was highlighted by critics for adding emotional texture to the storyline. The music was praised in online film reviews and aired on JioSaavn and Apple Music prominently after release.
  • Independent Artist Spotlight – Amazon Music India
    As an independent Bengali playback and fusion artist, Arnab Banerjee was part of Amazon Music India's "Indie Voices of the East" curation in late 2023. This included a profile on the app's homepage and placement across featured playlists.
