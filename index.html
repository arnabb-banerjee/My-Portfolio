Arnab Banerjee

Playback Singer · Composer · Lyricist

Welcome to the official website of Arnab Banerjee (Indra), a Bengali playback singer, independent composer, and indie artist from Kolkata. Known for soulful expression and versatility, Arnab’s songs are featured in Bengali films and available on Spotify, YouTube, Apple Music, and JioSaavn.

With a passion for creating heartfelt melodies and modern arrangements, Arnab bridges the world of Bengali cinema music and independent originals. His work ranges from romantic ballads to fusion experiments, blending traditional Bengali roots with global sounds. Whether performing playback for movies or releasing original singles, Arnab continues to connect with audiences through music that speaks directly to the soul.

Recognition & Achievements of Arnab Banerjee – Bengali Playback Singer & Indie Artist

Top 5 Emerging Bengali Vocalists

Kolkata FM – 2023

In 2023, Arnab Banerjee, also known as Indra, was honored as one of the top 5 emerging Bengali playback singers by Kolkata FM 93.5. His soulful performance of “Ei Shohor” ran for three consecutive weeks, earning high audience engagement.

Guest Performer on Doordarshan Bangla

National Cultural TV Appearance

Arnab Banerjee was featured on Doordarshan Bangla’s cultural program “Tarana.” He showcased original Bengali compositions and a tribute to Rabindranath Tagore, broadcast nationally to millions of viewers.

Featured Artist – Spotify Rising India

Independent Music Achievement

His single “Ei Shohor” was included in Spotify’s Rising India Playlist, crossing over 120,000 streams in 3 weeks. This recognition placed Arnab among India’s most promising Bengali indie artists.

Playback Acknowledgement – Pora Baanshi

Film Industry Recognition (2021)

In the Bengali film “Pora Baanshi”, Arnab Banerjee’s playback singing was highly praised by critics and featured prominently on JioSaavn and Apple Music.

Indie Artist Spotlight – Amazon Music India

Independent Artist Recognition

In 2023, Amazon Music India featured Arnab Banerjee as part of its “Indie Voices of the East” campaign. His work was highlighted on playlists and the Amazon Music homepage.

Arnab Banerjee

Bengali Playback Singer · Composer · Indie Artist

Arnab Banerjee, artistically known as Indra, is a passionate Bengali playback singer, independent composer, and rising indie music artist from Kolkata. Recognized for his soulful voice, classical foundation, and versatile style, Arnab bridges tradition with innovation in every performance. With 45+ official releases across Spotify, Amazon Music, Apple Music, JioSaavn, and YouTube, his music resonates with both mainstream and indie audiences worldwide.

🎶 Musical Journey

Born and raised in Kolkata, Arnab trained in Hindustani classical music under distinguished gurus, developing a strong foundation in raga-based performance. His playback career includes notable Bengali films such as Pora Baanshi, Ami Rabindranath 2, and Panta Bhaater Biriyani. Songs like Ei Shohor and Swapna Puraner Deshe highlight his vocal range, emotional depth, and adaptability across genres.

🌟 Artistic Identity

Arnab’s style blends ghazal sensitivity, classical precision, and contemporary pop-folk energy. Whether in soulful ballads or modern fusion, his voice carries authenticity and passion that leave a lasting impact. His growing digital presence has connected him to thousands of listeners across India and the global Bengali diaspora.

🚀 Vision & Impact

With humility and artistic integrity, Arnab continues to explore new sounds while staying rooted in Bengali language and culture. His goal is clear: to create music that moves hearts, preserves heritage, and inspires future generations. Every track reflects his commitment to growth, storytelling, and emotional honesty.
