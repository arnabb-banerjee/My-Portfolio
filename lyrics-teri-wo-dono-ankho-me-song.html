Teri wo dono ankho me

Author / Composer / Singer: Arnab Banerjee Indra

Languages: Bengali, Hindi & English

English Version

                
(Teri wo dono ankho me jindegi mai lutaya)2
                (Kuch vi to manga nehi mai, teri bagar khuda se)2

                (Firbhi mai tere dil me khudko mai dikhna chahta hu)2

                (Mai to tere bagar yeh, ek din bhi kaat na paaye)2
